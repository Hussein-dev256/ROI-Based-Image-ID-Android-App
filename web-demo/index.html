Permissions & Privacy

Image Upload & Storage

We need access to your images to perform object recognition. Images are processed securely and are not stored permanently unless you choose to save them to history.

Privacy & Data Protection

Your images are sent to a secure cloud API for recognition. We do not use your images for training or share them with third parties. All processing happens in real-time and images are not retained by the API provider.

Optional Features

Recognition history is stored locally in your browser. You can clear it anytime. No account or login required.
